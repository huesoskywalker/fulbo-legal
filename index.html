Privacy Policy

Fulbo — Recreational Football Management App
Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account and use Fulbo, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, first name, last name, date of birth, and password (stored securely by our authentication provider)
  • Optional profile information: username, phone number, profile photo, and bio
  • Team and match data: teams you create or join, match results, attendance records, and player statistics
  • Device information: push notification tokens (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core app functionality: organizing matches, tracking attendance, and calculating player statistics
  • To send push notifications about matches and team activity (only if you opt in)
  • To display your profile to other members of your team

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase, which uses encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted on AWS infrastructure. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your profile information (name, username, avatar) is visible to members of teams you belong to. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed. Match statistics and historical records may be retained in anonymized form.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your personal information through the app at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Fulbo requires a date of birth during registration. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use of Fulbo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
